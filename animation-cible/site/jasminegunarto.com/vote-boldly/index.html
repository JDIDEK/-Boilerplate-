    Civic Nation’s “ALL IN Campus Democracy Challenge” helps first-time voters by making the process clearer and more accessible.

    Many young voters undecided: not from apathy, but from limited outreach and a lack of trusted information.
    Create an exciting/attention-grabbing animation through social media post that would appeal to young first time voters: to push them to get out and vote
    Always thinking back to the company (their colors for example) and enhancing upon this through bolder and more dynamic animation made the project interesting
    The design shifted to simplified collage after early drafts did not meet client expectations. Layered imagery and striking text now speak to young voters, highlighting the lasting impact of their voice
    ADDY Student Silver Award 2025
